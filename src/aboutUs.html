Overview

An initiative / Platform led by sgd developers with a goal to seek for seeds
for farmers for plantation in the aim to end zero hunger on the less privileged
ones during this pandemic time and beyond.

Goals

  1. Make food production as easy as possible.
  2. Reserved.

Specifications

Who can participate:

Everyone, Donate-A-seed is open to anyone who is willing to help donate
to farmers in contacting hunger.

Why should I participate:

To be part of a community that will reach out to millions of less privileged
people who needs food at this time and beyond
