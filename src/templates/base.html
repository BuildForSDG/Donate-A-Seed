    ABOUT US

    what is DONATE A SEED™?

    An initiative Platform led by SDG developers with a goal to seek for seeds for farmers for plantation in the aim to end zero hunger on the less privileged ones during this pandemic time and beyond.


    How does it work?

    When you a register as Farmer, you will receive aids from us by providing you Good Seeds, Assistance in starting up a farm and Improving your already built business, At the end of the harvest we then take 10% of the profit made use and it to provide food for the less priviledged ones.

    One can also register as a Seed Deliverer to help deliver seeds or resources to Farmers In need of them, after Successful delivery you get a compensation.

    How will we make this happen?

    Our main source of income is through Donations from kind hearted individuals who also want Hunger to end across Africa.

    For more information feel free to contact us
